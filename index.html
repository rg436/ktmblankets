Welcome

With an experience of more than 30 years in the business of blankets, we have dealt in and with, a fully integrated line of all kinds of blankets. Right from the more economic ones to the luxe , we know it all. We specialize in the trade of Mink and Polar Fleece blankets. Our production infrastructure is something that we've built over a long period of time, learning and experimenting along the way and we are proud of the team behind it. We welcome you and look forward to a fulfilling journey with you.

About Us

We are an India based firm, with a strategic location in Panipat, Haryana, the biggest hub of textiles in the country. Our manufacturing plant, M/s Raghav Woolen Mills, also located in Panipat, is one of the first mink manufacturing establishments in the country. We employ several hundered people at any time of the year, with the number significantly increasing during production heavy months. Most of our senior management has been with us for years and we're really proud of our team. We believe in ethical business and this is something we are known for throughout our business family. Our workspaces, including offices, display areas and production houses, are fully compliant with various standards defined by the government and renowned private bodies. We have always looked at our customers as a part of our family and we keep that in mind when we service them. We have been and are growing each passing day and our vision is to establish ourselves as a leading textile house in the country while maintaing our structural and ethical integrity.

SUSTAINABILITY



We realise the effects of an industrial unit on the environment. We are also familiar with various malpractices that have been known to occur in an industry setting in the past. However, we aim to change that stream of thought among people, and we realise that only by taking action can we set an example for the whole industry to use sustainable measures and fair terms.

Saving Water

Zero Liquid Discharge: We were the absolute first mink blanket manufacturing company in India to implement the zero liquid discarge technology, thereby recycling every last possible drop of water.

Human Rights

Our team is as much of a family to us as our families at home. We are really particular about the protection of each and every employee's human rights. We have been complianced with many social and governmental audits in this regard and we stand strong in our mission to make our industrial work sustainable.

Ethics In Business

Being one of the oldest firms in the trade, as well as being a family run business, we value ethics over absolutely any other aspect of the corporate ecosystem. We have a history of building long and strong relationships with our customers and suppliers.
